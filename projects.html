Java   Apache Thrift
  • Developed a distributed banking application to implement CHandy-Lamport Global Snapshot algorithm.
  • Multiple bank servers (instances of the application) are initialized with a random amount of balance.
  • Once initialized, these servers then start transferring random amount to each other at random intervals of time.
  • Thread synchronization was used to make sure money transfer messaged are processed in FIFO order.
  • Then a controller was designed to initiate a new snapshot at regular intervals with a goal to get an accurate snapshot of the amount held by each bank server as well as the amount in transit.
